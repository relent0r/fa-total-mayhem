Total Mayhem mod for SupCom Forged Alliance
by Burnie222


Installation:


Before installing this version you must delete any previous installations of Total Mayhem

Unzip in your C:\documents and settings\--your username--\My documents\My games\Gas powered games\supreme commander forged alliance\mods\   folder.
